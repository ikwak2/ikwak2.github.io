Research interests

I am interested in statistical genetics. I focus on developing methods and tools for detecting and identifying genes contributing to complex phenotypes. I am eager to design new methods for big and complicated data. I am also excited to develop new useable tools for the method so that those methods can be widely adopted and used by other researchers.

  • Genetic association testing for GWAS.
  • Statistical methods in single cell RNA sequencing data.
  • Quantitative trait loci mapping in experimental organisms.
  • Functional data analysis.
